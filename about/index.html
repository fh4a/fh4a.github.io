About us

We are a small (for now) group of queer and trans folks living in the SF Bay Area. We started our group in early 2025 in response to the attacks on the lives of trans folks. Through research and our own experiences, we decided that providing every trans person with free access to hormones was doable. Since then, we have been working to expand our reach and accomplish our mission.

Due to the current state of the country, the members of our group are remaining anonymous in order to safely provide services to those in need.
