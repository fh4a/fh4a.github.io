Free HRT Kitchen

Hello! We're a group of queer and trans folks in the SF Bay Area who are working ensure that all trans folks have access to the hormones they need. We do not ask for payment of any kind for the resources that we provide.

Are you a trans person who is having trouble accessing your hormones? Or do you know someone who is? Contact us and we will work to help.

Interested in supporting us? Check out our Supporters page for more info.
